SpaceX launched Bandwagon-1 from Kennedy Space Center, its first dedicated rideshare mission targeting mid-inclination orbits, distinct from Transporter missions. The Falcon 9 rocket deployed 11 satellites, including South Korea's "425 Project" and HawkEye 360's geolocation satellites. SLATS, led by JAXA, is testing high-speed laser communication in space. Equipped with laser terminals, the satellite aims to validate this technology's feasibility for future missions. It seeks to confirm data rates, signal stability, and performance under various conditions. Success could revolutionize satellite and deep space communication. JAXA collaborates globally to advance laser tech.
